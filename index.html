Welcome to the Pour House.

Hi I'm Sam! I am a Registered Nurse working at the University of Washington Medical Center. While on leave healing from a back injury, I needed a way to fill my new found free time. Instead of consuming copious amounts of Netflix and indulging in frivolous shopping sprees I stumbled upon an environmentally friendly alternative to traditional candle making. As a result, Pour House Candle Company was born!
